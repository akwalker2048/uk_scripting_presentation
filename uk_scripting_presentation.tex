\subsection{And...Do Something With a Script}
\begin{frame}[fragile]\frametitle{Loops}
  Sometimes, you need to loop through files and do something with them:
\begin{verbatim}
#!/bin/sh

for fname in *.png; do
    bname=$(basename -s .png $fname)
    convert $fname eps3:$bname.eps
done
\end{verbatim}
\end{frame}
